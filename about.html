Our History

Our Journey

Since our inception in 2004, Vision System and Solutions has been a pioneer in delivering reliable, affordable, and comprehensive IT and surveillance services. With a strong foundation built by our founders — Makarand Dhage, Hemant Maldonkar, and Vivek Hanchate — we have grown to become a trusted partner for individuals, businesses, and institutions alike.

Over the past two decades, we've evolved and expanded our services, constantly adapting to the changing technological landscape while maintaining our core values of excellence and customer satisfaction.

Today, we stand proud as a comprehensive technology solutions provider, equipped with the expertise and resources to address a wide spectrum of IT and security challenges for our diverse clientele.
