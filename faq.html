Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services. If you can't find what you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us directly.

What services does Vision Systems & Solutions provide?

We offer a wide range of services including computer and laptop repairs, hardware upgrades, CCTV installation and maintenance, as well as onsite, remote, and verbal IT support for individuals and businesses.

Do you offer home or office visits for repairs and installations?

Yes, we provide onsite services for both homes and offices. Our technicians will visit your location to diagnose and fix the issue or install new systems like CCTV setups.

How do I book a service with Vision Systems & Solutions?

You can book a service by calling us directly, messaging us on our official WhatsApp number, or visiting our office. We also offer remote assistance in case you are unable to visit physically.

Can you help with software issues as well as hardware problems?

Absolutely. We handle both hardware and software issues, including virus removal, OS installation, driver updates, data recovery, and more.

What areas do you service?

We provide services throughout the local area and surrounding regions. For remote support, we can assist clients anywhere with an internet connection. Please contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

Do you offer warranties on repairs and installations?

Yes, we stand behind our work with service warranties. The specific warranty period depends on the type of service provided. All new hardware components come with their manufacturer warranties, and our labor is guaranteed as well.
